# Wealth Management — AssetMark Model Selection

## Purpose & scope
Document and approve the AssetMark model or strategy selected for a client.
**In scope:** documenting and recording an AssetMark model selection/change and the **advisor
approval** of it. **Out of scope:** proposal generation (`WLTH-SOP-04`). This is an **operational**
procedure — it is not investment advice and defines no suitability standard.

## Externally governed — fees & investment suitability
The **advisory fee schedule** and any **investment-suitability determination/standard** are
**externally governed** — see current **Advisory Agreement** / **Form ADV Part 2A** and the firm's
advisory/compliance process. This SOP records the **operational selection and advisor approval** only;
it does **not** define fees or a suitability rule. *(Controlled citations will be added during the
Compliance Validation milestone.)*

## Audience
Lead Advisor (selects/approves the model per client objectives); Operations (documents the selection).

## Prerequisites
- The client's documented risk tolerance, income needs, and time horizon are available.

## Required systems & permissions
- **AssetMark eWealthManager**; Wealthbox. `SME CONFIRMATION REQUIRED`: confirm the current AssetMark
  platform and model-selection workflow.

## Procedure
1. Review the client's **documented** risk tolerance, income needs, and time horizon.
2. Confirm investment objective and account purpose.
3. Review available AssetMark model options.
4. Confirm whether the model supports the client's long-term and tax-aware planning needs.
5. **Document the advisor rationale** for the selected model.
6. Confirm fees and investment constraints **per the governing documents** (see *Externally governed*).
7. **Obtain advisor approval before implementation.**
8. Save model-selection notes to the client file (by reference).
9. Update the proposal / account-opening / model-change workflow.

> ⚠️ **CAUTION.** Do **not** implement a model until **advisor approval is documented** (per LL-002,
> model selection requires advisor confirmation).

## Expected results
A documented, advisor-approved model selection with recorded rationale, reflected in the proposal or
account setup.

## Validation & evidence
Risk tolerance documented; model rationale documented; advisor approval recorded; setup reflects the
approved model.

## Troubleshooting
| Symptom | Likely cause | Resolution | Escalate if |
|---|---|---|---|
| Model implemented without rationale | Notes skipped | Require model notes before implementation | Already implemented |
| Model ≠ risk profile | Not compared | Compare risk profile before approval | Advisor disagrees |
| Advisor approval missing | Step skipped | Do not implement until approval documented | — |

## Escalation
Escalate model/risk mismatches to the **Lead Advisor** (Michael Shelton) before implementation.
